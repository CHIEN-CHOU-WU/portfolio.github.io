AI Envirenment for Camelot

  • View Project

AI can be used to enhance automation and help eliminate the necessity for humans to perform tedious tasks. Writing Non-Player Characters (NPCs) logic can be a very fun and creative process, but it can sometimes take a very long time. So why not develop and train AI models that can survive in real life-like scenarios, visualize the simulations by using game engines, and at the same time study their actions?

Project Overview:

Create a fully automated ecosystem of NPCs using Reinforcement Learning. With the aid of reinforcement learning, create multiple agents or models for each NPCs to automatically conduct various tasks and create settings similar to human behaviors like satisfying basic needs like hunger, thirst, and energy. These agents will be trained on simulations of states and actions which will allow the NPCs to act on their own will. The goal is to create agents or models that allow the NPCs to act on their own without any written instructions and ultimately creating an ecosystem with no outside guides.

Once an ecosystem is established with fully automated NPCs, multiple layers can be introduced such as a trading system, distribution of wealth, each of the NPCs happiness, establishing supply and demand, different skills and traits for the NPCs, and etc. When different layers are introduced, a study can be done to see the effects of each event to the ecosystem.

About the Project

Studying economics enables us to understand past, future and current models, and apply them to societies, governments, businesses and individuals.

Simulate a virtual economy similar to real life, create AI models to survive in this ecosystem, and study how AI models behave with different economic conditions.

AI Agents

AI Agents are trained in simulated environments 1 million iteration per model. All models continuously learn in deployed environment

Occupations

    Different skills allows NPCs to do better in certain occupations:
  • No skills
  • Produce more food
  • Take advantage of profit margins
  • Get paid more during worker shortage
  • Get paid more wages for working

Deep Reinforcement Learning Model

Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) Model. Learns from a small batch of experience.

Reward

0.5 per second alive + happiness
Happiness = health + hunger + energy + wealth [-3 to 3]

Goals

  • Satisfy hunger and energy.
  • Maintain health.
  • Accumulate wealth.
  • Change occupations based on economic conditions.
  • To make the reward as high as possible.
